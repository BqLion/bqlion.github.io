TASK1_回答结构与评分标准

TASK1题型

  • Agree or disagree

    问你所有十六岁一下的小孩都必须得去学校，你同意不同意？

  • 二选一

    some people prefer to do A,others prefer to do B, what`s your opinion?

  • suggestion部分

    学校做出了一个新政策，比如给本学院的学生们开设了一个自习室，问你的suggestion是什么

一般考察模式是机器读完材料后，给你15秒的准备时间，然后给你45秒的时间作答。

回答结构

MTE（A&C）

  • Major point

    总观点，建议可以直接读题

  • Topic sentence

    分论点

  • Explanation

    解释，是对分论点的解释和说明，这里如果能说很多把时间充起来，就可以免去后边的A&C步骤

  • Anacdote

    举例论证，如果上文解释不到位，这里就举具体例子论证，目的是为了拖时间

  • Comparison

    对比论证，仍然是为了拖时间

演示论证结构（freestyle的时候才需要这种结构）

题目：

With the development of technology,like electronic books,libraries will be replaced.
Do you agree or disagree?

总的论证结构就是，首先M亮明总观点，是同意还是不同意，然后T分论点，说的具体一点，同意他的什么，不同意的话不同意他的什么。然后是E解释，对自己的分论点做一些解释。如果时间不够的话，就在解释的基础上加一些A例证或者C对证。

M1(总观点1)：

Well I don`t think that with the development of technology,like electronic books,libraries will be replaced.

M2(总观点2)：

Well I don`t agree with the statement that with the development of technology,like electronic books,libraries will be replaced.

T(分论点)

That`s because I think libaries are actually good places for students to study besides providing a place for students to read or checkout books.

E(解释) - 解释分论点的时候，如果时间多就用三件套（解释+例证+对证）

I mean it`s very quiet there,so students could focus on the things that they are dealing with like reading books,writing papers or preparing for exams and stuff like that.So they could finish the assignments quickly.

A（例证）

For instance,I prepared for my finals at the library  last semester and there I was able to review the textbooks,do problem sets and go my class notes in just 2 days which was faster than studying in my dorm.And then I was ready for the upcoming tests.

C（对证）

But other places are not so ideal for study,take dorms for instance,it will be noisy there since some people might play the guitar or some of them might talk loudly on the phone.Therefore, students who want to study there might get distracted easily.

段子活用（段子能用就不需要组织结构了）

例一：

Some people preferto decorate their bedroom or studyrooms with alot of decorations like pitcures and stuff while others like to keep their surroundngs simple and free of decorations.Which do you prefer and why?

答：

既然问到了关于学习环境的问题，就可以使用“图书馆学习安静好”的段子。

例二：

Employees shouldn`t send personal texts or emails during work hours.Do you agree or disagree?

答案：既然是问在工作中是否可以发送个人的短信息，那么就牵扯到一个学习/工作效率问题，所以可以套用图书馆的段子。

例三：

The best way for parents to teach children good behavior is to praise their good behavior than to critize their bad behavior.
A/D ?

仍然可以套用如下的图书馆段子

如下

I think the best way for parents to teach children good behavior is to critize their bad behavior.
Because critizing their bad behavior can give them a lesson.
For example,when I was eight,I was once token by my mom to a public library,I was so exciting so I screaming and shouting,then my mom critizing hardly,and even punished me for not allowing me to watch my favorite TV series.
However,if you just praise a kid`s good behavior,they will do good behavior more ofen,but they will never know something they behave wrongly.

评分标准

现在还是人工打分为主，AI打分为辅。

AI主要评估的是你的Fluency流利度，人工主要评价的是你的逻辑能力。

AI Fluency 高分秘诀：

  • 语速适中
  • 可以有适当的停顿
  • 少重复

练习方法：模仿跟读

AI Pronunciation 高分秘诀

  • 重音准确
  • 发音标准

练习方法：siri识别

Al Vocabulary秘诀

  • 用词准确
  • 用词、句形多样化
